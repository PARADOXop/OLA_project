OLA Project


Problem statement: OLA, transformed urban mobility by offering convenience and affordability to millions of users. 
                   while doing this they generate vast amounts of data related to ride booking, driver avaibility, fare calculations, customer preferences, etc.
                   however deriving actionable insights from this data remains a challenge. to enhance operational efficiency, improve customer satisfaction and
                   optimize business strategies. this project focuses on analyzing OLA's ride-sharing data
